About me

Hi I am François. I am half German and half French. My Spanish speaking friends however call me Pancho.

Since I got my first computer I was interested in building websites and later mobile apps and games. When I am not working, I hit the gym or other people in self-defense classes and don't regret spending most of my money for traveling. I have relocated about 25+ times, but only across 10 cities and 4 countries. Currently I live in Zurich. My favorite place on earth: Madeira.
