I am the founder of dibulo
- an interactive coloring experience for kids

Dibulo started as a prototype for an interactive coloring experience designed for kids, blending creativity and technology. To develop the idea further, I joined an accelerator program in Madeira, where I refined the product. To this day there are different coloring worlds for kids to explore. Dibulo's ease of setup and use — requiring no additional hardware — made it especially appealing to parents, teachers, and kindergartens. Its unique approach to engaging young minds helped it gain traction, ultimately achieving bestseller status on Amazon.nl and becoming a beloved tool in early childhood education.
